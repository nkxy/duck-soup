About Me

Why design?

My passion of tech comes from the potential it has to positively impact people's lives. As I gained experience as a developer and a product manager, I realized what I loved most was solving problems that are close to the user, which led me to UX design.

I love how supportive the design community is, and how feedback and communication are essential. Working as a UX designer, I enjoy the balance between understanding & applying UX principles with more creative designs. I find myself constantly pushing to improve how I work through the design process, and the results in the final product I create.
